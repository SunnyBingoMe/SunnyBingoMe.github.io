  • Trying a less saturated processing here.
    Trying a less saturated processing here. It's also earlier, so they sky really hadn't turned to fire yet.

    Clean 1 preset in HDR Efex Pro, and then tweaked.
    Color Efex 4 using ND Grad, Brightness/Warmth (to cool it), and one other I don't remember.
    Viveza to up the brightness/saturation of two trees in the distance and lower the saturation of the hot spot in the middle.
    Dfine because it's an HDR shot at 1600 ISO. And sharpened.
